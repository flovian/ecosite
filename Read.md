EcoSite: Right Place, Right Industry, Sustainable Future
Overview
EcoSite is an AI-powered mobile application designed to help industries and planners select environmentally sustainable locations for industrial development. It analyzes environmental and social factors to ensure economic growth does not harm communities or ecosystems.
The platform bridges the gap between industrial expansion and environmental responsibility by providing data-driven insights for better decision-making.

Problem Statement
Industrial development is essential for economic growth, yet many industries are established without proper environmental and social impact analysis. This exposes communities to polluted air and water, increases health risks, and damages ecosystems.
Urban planners and industry developers currently lack accessible, data-driven tools to evaluate environmental risks such as population density, pollution levels, and ecological sensitivity before selecting industrial sites.
How might we use Artificial Intelligence to analyze environmental and geographic data to help decision-makers choose safer, more sustainable industrial locations while supporting Sustainable Development Goal 11 (Sustainable Cities and Communities) and Sustainable Development Goal 13 (Climate Action)?

General Objective
To develop a mobile application that assists industries in identifying environmentally sustainable locations by providing real-time environmental data, pollution indicators, and guidance on best practices, thereby promoting responsible industrial development and minimizing environmental impact.

Sustainable Development Goals
EcoSite supports:


Sustainable Development Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities


Sustainable Development Goal 13: Climate Action



AI Assistance
Artificial Intelligence is used in EcoSite to:


Analyze environmental data such as air and water quality


Process geographic and satellite data for site evaluation


Assess population density and proximity to residential areas


Predict pollution risks and environmental impact


Generate recommendations for the most suitable industrial locations


This allows faster, more accurate, and data-driven decision-making.

Solution
EcoSite is a smart industry siting application that:


Evaluates environmental sensitivity of locations


Analyzes population density


Assesses pollution risks


Recommends sustainable locations for industrial development



Methodology
1. Research and Data Collection


Identify key environmental factors (air quality, water quality, proximity to residential areas, protected zones)


Collect data from government databases, GIS maps, satellite data, and pollution reports


Review existing tools for insights


2. Requirement Analysis
Functional Requirements:


Display locations with environmental ratings

Non-Functional Requirements:


User-friendly interface


Fast performance


Offline accessibility


3. App Design


Create UI/UX wireframes


Design database for environmental and location data


Plan system architecture


4. App Development


Built using Antigravity platform


Integrate interactive maps


Visualize environmental data


Implement AI recommendation system


5. Testing and Evaluation


Conduct user testing


Evaluate usability and accuracy


Improve based on feedback


6. Deployment and Maintenance
